# Hardware Requirements

## Minimum Requirements

Processor:
- Intel Core i3 or equivalent

RAM:
- 4 GB

Storage:
- 10 GB free space

Internet:
- Required for downloading dependencies and cloning the repository.

## Recommended Requirements

Processor:
- Intel Core i5 or above

RAM:
- 8 GB or more

Storage:
- SSD with at least 20 GB free space

About This Project

This tool predicts a country's Human Development Index (HDI) Category — Low, Medium, High, or Very High — from four core indicators used by the UNDP methodology: life expectancy at birth, mean years of schooling, expected years of schooling, and Gross National Income (GNI) per capita. A machine learning model was trained on historical development data, comparing several algorithms and automatically selecting the strongest performer.
